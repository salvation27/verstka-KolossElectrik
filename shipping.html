Shipping
Sending goods to any city by a carrier convenient for you:
Delivery terms:
Based on our experience, our clients receive the goods within 1-3 days. You can find out about the delivery time of goods from Moscow to your settlement on the websites of courier services.
Delivery cost and payment:
The cost of delivery of the goods will depend on the distance between Moscow and the destination, as well as on the weight and volume of the cargo. We will know the exact cost of delivery only after we have already sent you the ordered goods. You will pay for delivery upon receipt of the goods.

Dispatch Notice:
We notify our buyers when the goods are dispatched. Dispatch notification is made by email or telephone.
Arrival Notice:
When the goods arrive in your locality, an SMS will be sent to the specified mobile phone of the recipient with a notification of the arrival of the goods. An SMS message will come from the courier service of transportation and to your e-mail, which was specified by you when placing an order.
Go to the courier service department to pick up the goods after receiving an SMS with a notification of the arrival of the goods.
It should be noted that, unfortunately, SMS about the arrival of the cargo does not always reach. Therefore, if more than three days have passed since the day the goods were dispatched, and you have not yet received the notification of the arrival of the goods, then there is a reason to worry about the fate of your parcel.
